Onatah Eco Salon & Wigs

Onatah Eco Salon & Wigs is a southern Minnesota business that is located in the heart of the city of Mankato. The word ‘Onatah’ means “daughter of the earth” in Native American-Iroquois language. Rachel designed the logo with the mandala concept for the eco-salon side of the business.
