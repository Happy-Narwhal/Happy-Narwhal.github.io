Our Development Process

  • Meet

  • Plan

  • Develop

  • Launch

Gather Requirements

First we need to understand your brand, your goals and expectations for the project. We'll cover additional details of our development process and look to understand project scope as well as we can.

Plan

We'll make a plan of action and discuss with you which technologies would be best for your project. Depending on your project needs we may bring in additional developers. At HappyNarwhal, we're good at finding the right people to work with. We'll likely set up a Jira or Trello board for sharing more of our workflow with you.

Develop

Regardless of how we approach project management in working together, you're sure to get regular progress updates from us. We'll still be working together regularly as we make decisions on project details, implementing features, and anything else that arises.
