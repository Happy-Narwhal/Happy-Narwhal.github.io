Training & Workshops

Empowering teams with better tools, smarter workflows, and practical AI integration.

Hands-On, Custom-Fit Training

I offer workshops and team training sessions that bring immediate, real-world value to developers and startup teams. Whether you're learning to integrate AI into your workflows or refining how your team ships code, I focus on making the complex feel manageable—and actionable.

Popular Topics

  • Git Workflow Deep Dive: From solo projects to team collaboration, learn to use Git confidently. Topics include branching strategy, rebasing, pull request flow, and resolving merge conflicts without fear.
  • Kaizen for Developers: Apply the philosophy of continuous improvement to your codebase and workflow. Small, thoughtful process tweaks can compound into major performance and morale boosts.
  • AI-Assisted Development: Learn how to integrate AI tools like ChatGPT or GitHub Copilot into your development practice without sacrificing quality or understanding.
  • Code Reviews & Pair Programming: How to give and receive feedback that actually helps—and how to make peer collaboration a strength, not a bottleneck.

Who It's For

My training is especially useful for:

  • Startups building or scaling their first development team
  • Small dev teams looking to work more efficiently
  • Founders and PMs who want to understand modern dev culture and tooling
  • Organizations exploring how AI can augment—not replace—their developers

Format & Pricing

Trainings are available as one-time sessions, ongoing workshops, or custom-built programs based on your needs. Sessions can be held remotely or on-site.

Rates: Training starts at $150/hr. Discounted pricing available for multi-session packages or long-term engagements.
