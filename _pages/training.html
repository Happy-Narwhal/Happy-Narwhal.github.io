Training & Workshops

Empowering teams with better tools, smarter workflows, and practical AI integration.

Topics Covered

  • Git Workflow Deep Dive: From solo projects to team collaboration, learn to use Git confidently. Topics include branching strategy, rebasing, pull request flow.
  • Kaizen for Developers: Apply the philosophy of continuous improvement to your codebase and workflow. Small, thoughtful process tweaks can compound into major performance and morale boosts.
  • Programming Environment Optimization: Custom git hooks, code linting, bash shortcuts, and automation
  • AI-Assisted Development: Learn how to integrate AI tools like ChatGPT or GitHub Copilot into your development practice without sacrificing quality or understanding.

Who It's For

My training is especially useful for:

  • Startups building or scaling their first development team
  • Small dev teams looking to work more efficiently
  • Founders and PMs who want to understand modern dev culture and tooling
  • Organizations exploring how AI can augment—not replace—their developers
