Outsourcing & Remote Talent

Get connected with motivated remote developers and virtual assistants. Whether you're building a product, need help scaling operations, or want to incorporate AI into your workflow, we can help you find the right people for the job.

Why Outsource With Us?

Spending much of my time in the Philippines allows me to build strong local networks and connect you directly to junior developers, programming interns, and virtual assistants.

Significant Cost Savings:

You can expect to pay 4-8x less compared to US-based developers. Virtual assistant rates typically range from $1.50/hr to $3.50/hr, depending on English fluency and relevant experience.

AI-Enhanced Support:

Virtual assistants can be trained to help you with data entry or use AI to generate content like images, music, and copy — perfect for game development, e-commerce, or marketing workflows.

Flexible Engagements:

Whether you need help with sourcing, onboarding, or ongoing management, I can offer different levels of support. Please reach out for more details and custom arrangements.

What to Expect

Outsourcing can unlock significant advantages, but it comes with unique challenges. Setting the right expectations is key to success.

  • Language Proficiency: Communication skills vary—some candidates have basic proficiency while others are near-fluent. Clear communication and patience are essential.
  • Educational Gaps: Local education quality can differ from Western standards. Junior devs may need more coaching to meet your quality benchmarks.
  • Strong Leadership is Key: Remote junior developers thrive when paired with clear leadership, good processes, and regular oversight. This is where I can advise or help build your internal systems to support them.
